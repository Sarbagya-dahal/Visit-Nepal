Discover Nepal

A land of breathtaking mountains, rich culture, and unforgettable adventures.

Top Destinations

Pokhara

Pokhara is a vibrant city in Nepal, known for its stunning lakes, panoramic views of the Annapurna mountain range, adventure activities, and its tranquil ambiance as a gateway to popular trekking routes.

Ghandruk

A picturesque mountain village in Nepal, renowned for its stunning views of the Annapurna and Machhapuchhre peaks, rich Gurung culture, and its charm as a popular trekking destination.

Panch Pokhari

Panch Pokhari is a serene and sacred site in Nepal, featuring five pristine alpine lakes nestled amidst the Langtang region, revered for its natural beauty and spiritual significance among Hindu and Buddhist pilgrims.

Chitwan National Park

A UNESCO World Heritage Site known for wildlife viewing including rhinos, Bengal tigers, and elephants.

Lumbini

The birthplace of Lord Buddha, is a sacred UNESCO World Heritage site in Nepal, renowned for its serene monasteries, ancient ruins, and spiritual significance. It is a center of spiritual and historical significance, offering a glimpse into Buddhist heritage and culture.

Kathmandu

Kathmandu, the vibrant capital of Nepal, is a bustling city steeped in history, culture, and spiritual significance, surrounded by the majestic Himalayas.

Popular Treks

Everest Base Camp

Nestled in the breathtaking Himalayas of Nepal, serves as a gateway for trekkers and climbers seeking to experience the majestic beauty of Mount Everest up close.

Annapurna Trek

A world-renowned journey in Nepal that takes adventurers through lush valleys, terraced fields, charming villages, and breathtaking mountain vistas, offering a close encounter with the majestic Annapurna massif.

Langtang Trek

A serene and less-crowded journey in Nepal, leading trekkers through pristine forests, alpine meadows, and traditional Tamang villages, with stunning views of the Langtang Himalayas.

Mardi Trek

A short yet scenic adventure in the Annapurna region, offering breathtaking views of Machapuchare and the Himalayas.

Adventure Sports

Rafting

An exhilarating adventure sport that involves navigating rivers and rapids in an inflatable raft, offering a perfect blend of adrenaline, teamwork, and breathtaking views of nature.

Bungee Jumping

A thrilling adventure activity where participants leap from a bridge, while secured by an elastic cord, experiencing an adrenaline-pumping free fall and rebound.

Paragliding

An exciting aerial adventure sport that allows participants to glide through the skies using a lightweight, free-flying paraglider, offering breathtaking views and a sense of ultimate freedom.

Swing Jump

Swing Jump at the Cliff offers an exhilarating leap off a rugged precipice, blending adrenaline-pumping adventure with breathtaking views of the wild landscape.
